Real Madrid 1–2 Arsenal
2024–25 UEFA Champions League – Quarter-Final (Second Leg), Santiago Bernabéu, Madrid – 16 April 2025

Arsenal sealed progression to the semi-finals with a strong away performance to complete a 5–1 aggregate win over holders Real Madrid. After a convincing 3–0 first-leg victory at the Emirates Stadium, Arsenal continued their momentum in Madrid. Bukayo Saka broke the deadlock in the 65th minute with a composed finish after linking play inside the box and gave Arsenal firm control of the tie. Real Madrid responded in the 67th minute when Vinícius Júnior capitalised on a defensive hesitation to score, briefly giving the hosts hope of a comeback. Arsenal restored their lead in stoppage time, with Gabriel Martinelli netting a decisive goal in the 90+3rd minute to secure the 2–1 win on the night. The result confirmed Arsenal’s 5–1 aggregate victory and booked their place in the Champions League semi-finals.  ￼
