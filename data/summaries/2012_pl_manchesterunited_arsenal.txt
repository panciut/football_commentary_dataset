Arsenal 1–2 Manchester United
2011–12 Premier League, Emirates Stadium, London – 22 January 2012

Manchester United took the lead in the 45′+1 minute when Antonio Valencia headed in from a cross to give the visitors the advantage before half-time.
Arsenal levelled in the 71st minute as Robin van Persie finished from inside the area after a team-move to make it 1–1.
In the 81st minute, Danny Welbeck struck from close range to restore Manchester United’s lead and secure the 2–1 win.
