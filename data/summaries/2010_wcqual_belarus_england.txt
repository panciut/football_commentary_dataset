England 3–0 Belarus
2010 FIFA World Cup Qualifying – UEFA Group 6, Wembley Stadium, London – 14 October 2009

Peter Crouch put England ahead in the 4th minute with a close-range finish, giving the hosts an early lead.
England doubled their advantage in the 59th minute when Shaun Wright-Phillips scored with a low shot after sustained pressure in the attacking third.
Crouch added his second of the match in the 76th minute, converting from close range to complete the scoring.
