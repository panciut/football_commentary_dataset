Burnley 2–0 Sunderland
Premier League, Turf Moor – 23 August 2025

Burnley earned a 2–0 home victory over Sunderland in this early Premier League meeting, securing their first league win of the season with a strong second-half performance. After a tight opening half in which Sunderland looked competitive and had a goal ruled out for offside, Burnley broke the deadlock shortly after the break. Josh Cullen curled a well-struck effort into the bottom corner in the 47th minute, giving the hosts the lead and shifting momentum in their favour. Sunderland sought a response but were unable to finish their chances. Burnley doubled their advantage late in the match when Jaidon Anthony finished low from inside the box in the 88th minute after an incisive through ball from Cullen, sealing the 2–0 win. Sunderland pressured in stoppage time but could not find a way past Burnley’s defence, leaving Turf Moor with no goals. The victory provided Burnley a positive start to their Premier League campaign.  ￼
