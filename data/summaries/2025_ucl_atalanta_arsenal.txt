talanta 0–0 Arsenal
2024–25 UEFA Champions League – Group Stage, Gewiss Stadium, Bergamo – 19 September 2024

Atalanta and Arsenal played out a goalless draw in their UEFA Champions League fixture, with neither side able to break the deadlock across 90 minutes. The match featured few clear scoring opportunities, and both teams defended well and created limited chances in the attacking third. Arsenal goalkeeper David Raya was decisive, notably saving a penalty from Mateo Retegui in the 51st minute, denying Atalanta a breakthrough. Several Atalanta attempts — including efforts from Cuadrado — tested Arsenal’s defence but failed to find the target, while Arsenal’s forward players had sporadic opportunities that were kept out by good defending. The match finished 0–0, with both sides sharing the points in the group.  ￼
