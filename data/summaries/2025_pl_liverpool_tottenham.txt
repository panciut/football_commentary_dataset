Liverpool 5–1 Tottenham Hotspur
Premier League, Anfield – 27 April 2025

Liverpool claimed a commanding 5–1 victory over Tottenham in this Premier League clash at Anfield, a match that also secured the 2024–25 title for the Reds. Tottenham stunned the hosts early, taking the lead in the 12th minute when Dominic Solanke met a corner with a header to put Spurs ahead. 

Liverpool responded quickly and decisively. In the 16th minute, Luis Díaz levelled with a simple finish following sustained pressure. Alexis Mac Allister then gave Liverpool the lead in the 24th minute, firing a powerful strike into the top corner. Cody Gakpo extended the advantage in the 34th minute with a composed finish prior to halftime. 

After the break, Mohamed Salah added a fourth in the 63rd minute, finishing low into the net after a sustained attack. A defensive error led to an own goal by Destiny Udogie in the 69th minute, completing the scoring and sealing a lopsided win for Liverpool.

Liverpool dominated after conceding early, overturning the deficit with five clear goals and celebrating clinching the Premier League title at Anfield.
