Juventus 2–0 Manchester City
2024–25 UEFA Champions League – League Phase, Allianz Stadium, Turin – 11 December 2024

Juventus secured a notable 2–0 victory over Manchester City in their Champions League league-phase meeting at the Allianz Stadium. After an evenly contested first half with few clear goalscoring opportunities, the match was decided in the second period.

The breakthrough came in the 53rd minute when Dušan Vlahović rose to head home from close range after sustained pressure and a precise cross, giving Juventus the lead. City sought to respond, pressing forward and creating openings, but Juve’s organised defence held firm.

Juventus doubled their advantage in the 75th minute with Weston McKennie, who struck a well-timed finish from inside the box following a linking move that opened space in City’s defence. Manchester City continued to push late on, including chances for Erling Haaland, but were unable to score. The match finished 2–0 in favour of Juventus.  ￼
