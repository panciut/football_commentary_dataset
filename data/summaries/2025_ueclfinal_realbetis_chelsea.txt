Real Betis 1–4 Chelsea
2025 UEFA Conference League Final, Stadion Wrocław, Wrocław – 28 May 2025

Chelsea overturned an early deficit to secure a 4–1 victory over Real Betis and lift the 2024-25 UEFA Conference League trophy. Real Betis took an unexpected lead in the 9th minute when Abde Ezzalzouli finished low into the bottom corner after a swift attacking move on the counter-attack. Betis maintained their advantage into half-time, despite Chelsea pressing for an equaliser.  ￼

The second half saw a transformed Chelsea performance. In the 65th minute, Enzo Fernández headed home from a Cole Palmer delivery to level the match. Just five minutes later (70′), Nicolas Jackson gave Chelsea the lead by bundling the ball over the line off another Palmer assist. Jadon Sancho extended the advantage in the 83rd minute with a curled finish, and Moisés Caicedo added a late fourth in 90+1′, sealing the comeback and the trophy for the London side.  ￼
