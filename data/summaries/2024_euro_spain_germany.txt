Spain 2–1 Germany (after extra time)
UEFA Euro 2024 – Quarter-Final, MHPArena, Stuttgart – 5 July 2024

Spain advanced to the Euro 2024 semi-finals with a dramatic extra-time victory over hosts Germany in a tightly contested quarter-final. After a goalless first half, Dani Olmo broke the deadlock in the 51st minute, steering a low finish beyond the goalkeeper to give Spain the lead. Germany resisted and gradually grew into the match, eventually forcing parity in the 89th minute when Florian Wirtz slammed in a low strike from inside the box to make it 1–1 and send the game into extra time.  ￼

With penalties looming late in extra time, Mikel Merino rose highest in the 119th minute to head home from Olmo’s cross, securing a dramatic 2–1 victory for Spain and a place in the semi-finals.  ￼
