Inter Milan 1–0 Arsenal
2024–25 UEFA Champions League – Group Stage, San Siro, Milan – 6 November 2024

Inter Milan earned a narrow 1–0 victory over Arsenal in their Champions League group-stage meeting at the San Siro. The only goal of the match came from the penalty spot just before half-time, when Hakan Çalhanoğlu converted confidently after Mikel Merino was adjudged to have handled inside the box, giving Inter the lead heading into the break. Arsenal dominated large periods of possession in the second half and registered numerous attempts on goal, forcing Inter goalkeeper Yann Sommer into several key saves, but were unable to find an equaliser. Despite Arsenal’s pressure and attempts late in the game, Inter’s defence held firm to preserve the lead and secure all three points.  ￼
