Juventus 1–4 Real Madrid
2017 UEFA Champions League Final, Millennium Stadium (Cardiff) – 3 June 2017

Real Madrid took the lead in the 20th minute, with Cristiano Ronaldo finishing low from inside the box after a swift attacking move to put the Spanish side ahead early.

Juventus responded seven minutes later. In the 27th minute, Mario Mandžukić scored a spectacular overhead kick from a cross, bringing the match level and energising the Italian side.

The second half saw Real Madrid seize control. Casemiro restored the lead in the 61st minute with a long-range strike that looped into the net, before Ronaldo added his second of the night in the 64th minute with a crisp finish after a well-worked move.

Juventus were reduced to ten men late in the game when Juan Cuadrado was sent off for a second yellow card in the 84th minute, further hindering their comeback efforts. Marco Asensio sealed the victory with a goal in the 90th minute, tapping in after Real Madrid’s sustained pressure.
