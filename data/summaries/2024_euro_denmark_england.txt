Denmark 1–1 England
UEFA Euro 2024 – Group C, Deutsche Bank Park, Frankfurt – 20 June 2024

England took the lead in their second group match when Harry Kane finished from close range in the 18th minute to put the Three Lions ahead after sustained pressure in the attacking third. England maintained possession and created several openings in the early phases.  ￼

Denmark responded before half-time. In the 34th minute, Morten Hjulmand scored with a powerful long-range strike into the bottom corner to equalise, cancelling out England’s lead and levelling the match.  ￼

The second half saw both teams push for a winner. England had opportunities, including a Phil Foden effort that struck the post, but neither side managed to score again. The match ended 1–1, with both teams sharing the points.  ￼
