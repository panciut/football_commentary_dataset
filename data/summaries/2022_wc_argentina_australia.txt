Argentina 2–1 Australia
2022 FIFA World Cup – Round of 16, Ahmad bin Ali Stadium, Al Rayyan – 3 December 2022

Argentina took the lead in the 35th minute when Lionel Messi finished with precision from inside the box to break the deadlock in a tight encounter. Argentina continued to press after half-time and doubled their advantage in the 57th minute when Julián Álvarez struck from close range after capitalising on a defensive lapse by Australia’s goalkeeper Mathew Ryan.

Australia fought back and halved the deficit in the 77th minute through an own goal by Enzo Fernández, when a shot from Craig Goodwin took a deflection and wrong-footed Argentina’s goalkeeper, setting up a tense final phase. Despite Australia’s late pressure and attempts to equalise, Argentina held firm to secure a 2–1 win and advance to the quarter-finals.

Key events:

35′ – Lionel Messi (Argentina) goal.

57′ – Julián Álvarez (Argentina) goal.

77′ – Enzo Fernández (Argentina) own goal (Australia).

This victory set up Argentina’s quarter-final clash with the Netherlands.
