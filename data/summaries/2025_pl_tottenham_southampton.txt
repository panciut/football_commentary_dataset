Tottenham Hotspur 3–1 Southampton
2024–25 Premier League, Tottenham Hotspur Stadium – 6 April 2025

Tottenham Hotspur secured a 3–1 home victory over Southampton in this Premier League match. Tottenham took the lead in the 13th minute when Brennan Johnson finished from close range after a low cross into the box, giving Spurs an early edge. Johnson doubled the lead in the 42nd minute with a composed finish from inside the penalty area following a well-worked team move.

Southampton pulled one back in the 90th minute when Mateus Fernandes scored with a close-range finish after good build-up play down the left flank, giving the visitors a late lifeline. However, Tottenham restored their two-goal cushion in stoppage time. In the 90+6th minute, Mathys Tel converted a penalty after a foul in the box to make it 3–1 and seal the win for the hosts.
