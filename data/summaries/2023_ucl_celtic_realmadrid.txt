Real Madrid 5–1 Celtic
2022–23 UEFA Champions League – Group Stage, Santiago Bernabéu, Madrid – 2 November 2022

Real Madrid secured a commanding victory over Celtic to finish top of Group F and maintain momentum in their Champions League campaign. The hosts took an early lead in the 6th minute when Luka Modrić converted a penalty after a foul in the box, giving Real an immediate advantage. Rodrygo extended the lead in the 21st minute, also scoring from the penalty spot after another defensive infraction.

Real Madrid continued to control the match after halftime, adding a third goal in the 51st minute when Marco Asensio finished from close range following sustained pressure. Vinícius Júnior made it 4–0 in the 61st minute with a composed strike after a quick attacking transition. Federico Valverde ensured the dominant result with a 71st-minute finish, rifling the ball home to cap Real’s scoring. Celtic had a consolation in the 84th minute when Jota found the net, but the deficit was already insurmountable.

Real Madrid’s attacking fluidity and clinical finishing saw them control possession and limit Celtic’s threat throughout, as the Scottish side struggled to stem the flow of chances against them. The 5–1 win confirmed Real’s position atop the group ahead of the final matchday.
