Fulham 0–1 Arsenal
Premier League, Craven Cottage – 18 October 2025

Arsenal secured a narrow 1–0 away victory in their Premier League fixture at Fulham. The decisive moment came in the 58th minute when Leandro Trossard finished from close range after a well-executed corner delivery, flicked on by Gabriel Magalhães, to give the Gunners the lead. Arsenal controlled the majority of possession and created more attacking opportunities, reflected in higher shot and pass statistics, but were unable to extend their advantage beyond the single goal. Fulham defended resolutely and had limited clear chances, with Arsenal goalkeeper David Raya largely untested. The match finished 0–1, with Arsenal holding on for the three points on the day.  ￼
