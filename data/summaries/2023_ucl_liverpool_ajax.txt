Liverpool 2–1 Ajax
2022–23 UEFA Champions League – Group A, Anfield, Liverpool – 13 September 2022

Liverpool claimed a dramatic late victory over Ajax in their Group A encounter at Anfield, earning all three points after an intense match. Mohamed Salah opened the scoring in the 17th minute with a powerful finish into the bottom corner, capitalising on Liverpool’s best build-up play of the first half. Ajax responded in the 27th minute through Mohammed Kudus, who curled a precise effort into the top corner after good link-up play down the left flank to level the scores.

The second half was competitive but lacked clear goalscoring moments, with Liverpool dominating possession and creating several half-chances while Ajax defended resolutely. Liverpool’s persistence was rewarded deep into stoppage time when Joël Matip rose highest from a corner to nod home the winning goal in the 89th minute, prompting a late celebration at Anfield and securing a crucial group stage win.

Key events:

17′ – Mohamed Salah (Liverpool) goal.

27′ – Mohammed Kudus (Ajax) goal.

89′ – Joël Matip (Liverpool) goal.

Liverpool’s late winner gave them momentum in the group and showcased their resilience under pressure.
