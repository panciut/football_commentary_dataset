Ecuador 1–2 Senegal
2022 FIFA World Cup – Group A, Khalifa International Stadium, Doha – 29 November 2022

Senegal secured a place in the World Cup Round of 16 with a come-from-behind win over Ecuador. After a balanced opening period, Ismaïla Sarr put Senegal ahead in the 44th minute from the penalty spot after being fouled in the box, giving the African side the lead just before half-time.

Ecuador responded early in the second half. In the 67th minute, Moisés Caicedo finished from close range after the ball was headed across the box, drawing the match level and briefly swinging momentum back to the South Americans.

Senegal regained the advantage in the 70th minute, when Kalidou Koulibaly volleyed home from a well-delivered set-piece, restoring the lead and ultimately deciding the match.

Despite late pressure from Ecuador, Senegal’s defence held firm to secure the 2–1 victory, ensuring qualification to the knockout stage.

Key events:

44′ – Ismaïla Sarr (Senegal) penalty goal.

67′ – Moisés Caicedo (Ecuador) goal.
