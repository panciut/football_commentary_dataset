Ukraine 0–4 England
UEFA Euro 2020 – Quarter-Final, Stadio Olimpico, Rome – 3 July 2021

England took an early lead inside the first few minutes when Harry Kane finished from close range in the 4th minute, turning in a through-ball after sustained pressure. England maintained control for much of the half, dictating tempo and creating openings.

Just after the break, Harry Maguire doubled England’s advantage in the 46th minute, heading home from a set-piece delivery. Kane struck again in the 50th minute, heading in to make it 3–0 and effectively put the tie beyond Ukraine’s reach.

England completed the scoring in the 63rd minute, when Jordan Henderson scored his first international goal by heading home from a corner, sealing a convincing 4–0 victory. The win saw England advance to the semi-finals of the tournament.
