Manchester City 3–0 Bayern Munich
2022–23 UEFA Champions League – Quarter-Final (First Leg), Etihad Stadium, Manchester – 11 April 2023

Manchester City took a commanding step toward the semi-finals with a strong home performance against Bayern Munich. The breakthrough came in the 27th minute, when Rodri curled a powerful strike from outside the box into the top corner to give City the lead. City’s dominance grew after the break, and in the 70th minute Bernardo Silva extended the advantage with a headed finish from close range following sustained pressure. Late in the game, Erling Haaland added a third for City in the 76th minute, volleying home after Bayern’s defence was stretched, sealing a convincing three-goal win at the Etihad Stadium.
