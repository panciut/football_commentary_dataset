Chelsea 2–1 Manchester City
2011-12 Premier League, Stamford Bridge, London – 12 December 2011

Manchester City took an early lead in the 2nd minute when Mario Balotelli finished after City’s incisive attacking move.
Chelsea equalised in the 34th minute through Raúl Meireles, who scored with a volley after good work down the right.
Late in the match, Frank Lampard converted a penalty in the 82nd minute to put Chelsea ahead and secure the win.

During the second half, Gaël Clichy of Manchester City was dismissed in the 58th minute after receiving a second yellow card.
