Liverpool 2–5 Real Madrid (Real Madrid win tie)
2022–23 UEFA Champions League – Round of 16 (first leg), Anfield, Liverpool – 21 February 2023

Liverpool started the tie brightly and stunned Real Madrid early with two quick goals. Darwin Núñez opened the scoring in the 4th minute with a clever flick to put Liverpool ahead, and Mohamed Salah doubled the lead in the 14th minute by capitalising on a defensive error from the visiting goalkeeper. Real Madrid responded strongly before the break, with Vinícius Júnior netting twice — first in the 21st minute and again in the 36th minute — to level the match after Liverpool’s blistering opening. Madrid carried their momentum into the second half. Éder Militão put the Spanish side ahead in the 47th minute with a headed finish from a set piece, and Karim Benzema extended the advantage with goals in the 55th and 67th minutes to complete the turnaround and consign Liverpool to a heavy defeat at Anfield. The five-goal haul put Real firmly in control of the tie heading into the second leg. (turn0search5

Second Leg – Real Madrid 1–0 Liverpool
15 March 2023 – Santiago Bernabéu, Madrid

In the return leg, Karim Benzema scored the only goal in the 78th minute to seal the tie for Real Madrid. Liverpool struggled to overcome the first-leg deficit, and Benzema’s late strike confirmed Real’s progression with a 6–2 aggregate win, ending Liverpool’s Champions League campaign that season.
