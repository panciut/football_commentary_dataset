Liverpool 2–5 Real Madrid
2022–23 UEFA Champions League – Round of 16 (First Leg), Anfield, Liverpool – 21 February 2023

Real Madrid produced one of the most remarkable comebacks in recent Champions League history at Anfield. Liverpool started the match explosively, racing into a 2–0 lead inside the first 15 minutes. Darwin Núñez put the hosts ahead in the 4th minute with a clever early finish, and Mohamed Salah doubled the advantage in the 14th minute by capitalising on a mistake from Real Madrid’s goalkeeper.
