Liverpool 3–1 Southampton
Premier League, Anfield – 8 March 2025

Liverpool overcame an early scare to secure a 3–1 home win over Southampton in the 2024-25 English Premier League season. Southampton stunned the hosts before half-time when William Smallbone finished from close range in added time of the first period to give the visitors the lead. Liverpool responded strongly after the break. In the 51st minute, Darwin Núñez equalised with a composed finish following sustained pressure. Shortly after, Mohamed Salah converted a 55th-minute penalty to put Liverpool ahead. Salah added a second penalty in the 87th minute, after a handball in the box, sealing the comeback and the victory. Liverpool controlled the second half and saw out the win to continue their title push.  ￼
