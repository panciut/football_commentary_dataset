Paris Saint-Germain 2–1 Arsenal
2024–25 UEFA Champions League – Semi-Final (Second Leg), Parc des Princes, Paris – 7 May 2025

Paris Saint-Germain sealed their place in the Champions League final with a 2–1 victory over Arsenal in the second leg of their semi-final tie, winning 3–1 on aggregate. PSG opened the scoring in the 27th minute when Fabián Ruiz struck from distance into the bottom corner after a defensive scramble in the box. Arsenal pushed for a response but were kept at bay by PSG keeper Gianluigi Donnarumma, who also saved a penalty in the 69th minute, preserving PSG’s lead. (turn0search1￼)

PSG doubled their advantage in the 72nd minute as Achraf Hakimi finished low after a quick combination on the counter-attack. Arsenal pulled one back in the 76th minute when Bukayo Saka scored with a low finish after good build-up play, but they could not find an equaliser before full-time. The match ended 2–1, confirming PSG’s progression to the Champions League final.
