Manchester United 2–3 Galatasaray
2023–24 UEFA Champions League – Group A, Old Trafford, Manchester – 3 October 2023

Galatasaray claimed a dramatic away victory at Old Trafford in a high-tempo group-stage match. Manchester United opened the scoring in the 17th minute when Rasmus Højlund headed in from close range after sustained early pressure. Galatasaray responded quickly, equalising in the 23rd minute through Wilfried Zaha, who finished calmly against his former club.

United regained the lead in the 67th minute, again through Højlund, who scored his second with another close-range finish. The momentum shifted almost immediately as Kerem Aktürkoğlu brought Galatasaray level in the 71st minute following a swift attacking move. United’s problems deepened when Casemiro was sent off in the 77th minute for a second yellow card.

Galatasaray completed the comeback in the 81st minute, when Mauro Icardi scored the decisive goal from inside the box. Despite late pressure, Manchester United were unable to respond, and the match ended 3–2 in favour of the Turkish side.
