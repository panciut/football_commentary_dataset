Uruguay 2–1 England
2014 FIFA World Cup – Group D, Arena Corinthians, São Paulo – 19 June 2014

Uruguay took the lead in the 39th minute when Luis Suárez headed in after a well-timed cross, giving his side the advantage before half-time.

England levelled the match in the 75th minute as Wayne Rooney finished from close range following a low cross into the box.

The winner came late in the game. In the 85th minute, Suárez struck again, this time after capitalising on a defensive lapse, firing past the goalkeeper to restore Uruguay’s lead.

Key events:
