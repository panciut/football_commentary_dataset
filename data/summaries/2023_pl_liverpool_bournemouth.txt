Liverpool 3–1 Bournemouth
2023–24 Premier League, Anfield – 19 August 2023

Bournemouth stunned Liverpool early, taking the lead in the 3rd minute when Antoine Semenyo capitalised on an early attack to finish past the goalkeeper. Liverpool responded as the game settled, gradually imposing their rhythm and creating opportunities against a disciplined Bournemouth side.

Liverpool drew level in the 28th minute through Luis Díaz, who finished low after a well-worked move on the left flank. Mohamed Salah then put Liverpool ahead in the 36th minute, converting from close range to reward sustained pressure. Despite being reduced to ten men when Alexis Mac Allister was sent off in the 58th minute for a high challenge, Liverpool continued to control the game.

The decisive third goal came in the 62nd minute as Diogo Jota converted after a swift transition, giving Liverpool a comfortable advantage. Bournemouth pushed for a way back but were unable to break down Liverpool’s defence in the closing stages.

Liverpool’s comeback from an early deficit secured an important early-season win in the Premier League at Anfield.
