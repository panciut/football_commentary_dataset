Croatia 4–1 Canada
2022 FIFA World Cup – Group F, Khalifa International Stadium, Al Rayyan – 27 November 2022

Canada started the match in spectacular fashion, scoring inside the 2nd minute when Alphonso Davies powered a header past Croatia’s goalkeeper — the first goal ever for Canada at a men’s World Cup.

Croatia responded decisively before half-time. In the 36th minute, Andrej Kramaric equalised with a low finish after a pass from Ivan Perisic, and Marko Livaja put Croatia ahead in the 44th minute with a precise drive from the edge of the area.

In the second half, Kramaric added his second of the game in the 70th minute, controlling a cross before firing low into the net to extend Croatia’s lead. Croatia wrapped up the scoring in stoppage time when Lovro Majer finished from close range after a swift counter-attack.

Key events:

Croatia’s comeback from an early deficit secured a decisive 4–1 victory and eliminated Canada from the tournament.
