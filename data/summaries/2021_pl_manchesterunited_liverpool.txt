Manchester United 2–4 Liverpool
Premier League, Old Trafford – 13 May 2021

Manchester United started strongly and took the lead in the 10th minute when Bruno Fernandes curled a shot into the net from distance, giving the hosts an early advantage.

Liverpool responded in the 34th minute as Diogo Jota finished instinctively from close range following sustained pressure and a ricochet in the box.
Just before half-time, Roberto Firmino put Liverpool ahead with a clinical header in stoppage time (45+3′), turning the tide firmly in Liverpool’s favour.

Liverpool extended their lead early in the second half with Firmino scoring again in the 48th minute after a quick attack. Marcus Rashford responded for United in the 68th minute, cutting the deficit with a composed finish.
In the 90th minute, Mohamed Salah sealed the victory with a late finish after a surge into the box, ensuring a 4–2 win for Liverpool.
