England 2–1 Slovakia (after extra time)
UEFA Euro 2024 – Round of 16, Veltins-Arena, Gelsenkirchen – 30 June 2024

England recovered from a first-half deficit to secure a dramatic extra-time victory and advance to the quarter-finals. **Slovakia opened the scoring in the 25th minute when Ivan Schranz finished with composure from inside the box to put the underdogs ahead. England struggled to create clear chances through much of the first half and early second period.  ￼

As the match approached full-time, England looked set for elimination until Jude Bellingham produced a stunning overhead kick in the 90+5th minute to level the score and send the tie into extra time.  ￼

Just after the restart of extra time, in the 91st minute, Harry Kane nodded in from close range after a knock-down inside the penalty area to give England the lead, which they held to secure a 2–1 victory.  ￼

Key events:
	•	25′ – Ivan Schranz (Slovakia) goal.  ￼
	•	90+5′ – Jude Bellingham (England) equaliser (overhead kick).  ￼
	•	91′ – Harry Kane (England) extra-time winner.  ￼
