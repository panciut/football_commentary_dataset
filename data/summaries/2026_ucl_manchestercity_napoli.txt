Manchester City 2–0 Napoli
2025–26 UEFA Champions League – Group Stage, Etihad Stadium, Manchester – 18 September 2025

Manchester City opened their Champions League campaign with a 2–0 home win over Napoli at the Etihad Stadium. The match was level at half-time, but Napoli were reduced to ten men early in the second period after Giovanni Di Lorenzo was sent off for a foul on Erling Haaland, leaving the visitors with a numerical disadvantage. City capitalised after the red card as the game opened up. In the 56th minute, Erling Haaland headed home from a Phil Foden assist, marking his 50th Champions League goal in record-time and giving the hosts the lead. City extended their advantage in the 65th minute when Jérémy Doku doubled the lead with a composed finish into the bottom corner after good approach play. Despite Napoli’s efforts to regroup, they were unable to create clear opportunities, and Manchester City controlled the remainder of the match to secure the win.  ￼
