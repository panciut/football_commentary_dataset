England 6–0 Andorra
2010 FIFA World Cup Qualifying – UEFA Group 6, Wembley Stadium, London – 10 June 2009

England opened the scoring in the 4th minute when Wayne Rooney headed home from close range to give the hosts an early lead.
Frank Lampard doubled the advantage in the 29th minute with a finish inside the box.
Rooney scored again in the 39th minute, extending England’s lead to 3–0 before half-time.

In the second half, Jermain Defoe struck twice in quick succession with goals in the 73rd and 76th minutes, increasing the margin.
Peter Crouch completed the scoring in the 81st minute with a close-range finish, sealing the 6–0 win.
